FAQ

What Blender versions are supported?

Preview-it™ is tested on Blender 4.0 to 4.5+ on macOS, Windows, and Linux. Blender 5.x support is in active development and will be rolled out during the beta.

Will Grease Pencil in Geometry Nodes be supported?

Yes. Grease Pencil in GN is already on the roadmap and will arrive as a free 1.x update before the full 1.0 launch.

Does it modify my node tree?

No. Preview-it™ is completely non-destructive. It evaluates your node trees in a controlled background context and draws overlays, but never inserts, deletes, or edits nodes, links or scene data.

Will it slow down Blender?

Previews run in a separate background worker with smart caching. You can limit resolution, lower refresh rate, or pause previews entirely if you’re pushing huge scenes — so you stay in control of performance.

Does it work with Repeat Zones and nested Node Groups?

Yes. Preview-it™ tracks attributes as they flow through Capture Attribute, Named Attribute, repeat zones, and nested node groups, so you can see how data evolves across your graph.
